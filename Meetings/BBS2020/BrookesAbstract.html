The GenApp framework was developed to meet the needs of CCP-SAS to easily bring a variety of SAS analysis and simulation software to the web. This framework is used to produce SASSIE-web and other SAS and non-SAS web portals or “science gateways”. Recently, many enhancements and improvements have been added to GenApp. This talk will provide a brief background on GenApp, overview deployed applications, and provide a summary of recent updates.
