Meetings and Workshops
2nd UK CCP-SAS Training Course
January 23-25, 2017
The Cosener's House, Abingdon-on-Thames, UK

This intensive course will provide participants with 'hands-on' 'user-level' instruction on how to use SASSIE-Web for their research, and we are very fortunate that Dr Joseph Curtis, the lead developer of SASSIE, has agreed to come from the US to deliver the core training.

We would also like to gratefully acknowledge the financial sponsorship for this Course provided by the STFC ISIS Pulsed Neutron & Muon Source and the Diamond Light Source which has allowed us to keep the registration fee down.

Registration costs ï¿½60/person, and includes 2 nights accommodation with evening dinner at The Cosener's House, Lunch on Days 1 & 2, and refreshments throughout. Windows PC's will be provided for participants to use.

Please Note: Due to the nature of the course, the number of participants is limited to 16.
